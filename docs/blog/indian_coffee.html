The Hitchhiker’s Guide to Coffee Available In The Indian Market¶

LAVAZZA IL Mattino Vivace¶

Score: 7/10 Price: 94.53/100 g

Has a plain profile. Un-offensive in taste. Dark in color. Great coffe for the price. Solid as a daily driver.

Colombian Brew Hazelnut¶

Score: 3/10 Price: 113.62/100g

Addition of flavor sacrifices the ‘coffee-ness’. There is a bit of hazelnut present but little to no coffee. It’s closer to being green tea than coffee.
